**Last updated:** 18 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how **CGB Technical Solutions** (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, stores, and discloses personal information when providing freelance software development, technical writing, documentation, consulting, and related services in Australia.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling personal information in accordance with the Australian Privacy Principles contained in the *Privacy Act 1988 (Cth)*.

## 1. What information we collect

We may collect personal information including:

* Name and business name
* Email address
* Phone number
* Billing and invoicing details
* Information submitted through contact forms
* Project requirements and communications
* Technical information such as IP addresses, browser type, and analytics data
* Any other information you voluntarily provide

We generally only collect information that is reasonably necessary to provide our services or communicate with you.

## 2. How we collect information

We may collect information when you:

* Submit a contact form on our website
* Email or contact us directly
* Engage us for services
* Subscribe to updates or newsletters
* Interact with our website

Information may also be collected automatically through standard web technologies such as cookies, server logs, and analytics tools.


## 3. How we use your information

We may use personal information to:

* Respond to enquiries
* Provide software development or technical writing services
* Manage projects and client relationships
* Send invoices and process payments
* Improve our website and services
* Maintain internal business records
* Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell personal information to third parties.

## 4. Contact forms and submissions

Information submitted through our website contact forms may be:

* Sent to our business email address
* Stored securely in internal systems or databases
* Used for project management or customer relationship management purposes

We take reasonable steps to protect submitted information from unauthorised access, misuse, or disclosure.

* Maintain website functionality
